# Student Portal User Manual: BrahmaVidya Galaxy

**Audience**: BrahmaVidya Students / Learners  

---

## Welcome to Your Spiritual and Philosophical Portal!
The **BrahmaVidya Student Portal** is designed to provide you with a distraction-free, mindful learning environment. Here is a guide to managing your learning experience.

---

## 1. Navigating Your Dashboard
When you log in, you will land on your **Student Dashboard**, which features three primary sections:
*   **Active Progress Streaks**: Highlights your consecutive daily learning habits. Make sure to complete at least one session daily to keep your streak going!
*   **Personal Notes Feed**: Lists recent study summaries and notebook entries you have captured.
*   **Interactive Study Goals**: Displays your custom milestones and real-time progress bars.

---

## 2. Managing Your Bookmarks
Found an inspiring video tutorial or text article? Bookmark it for easy access:
1.  Click the **Bookmark** icon located next to any lesson heading.
2.  Your Bookmarks list will instantly update with the item.
3.  To remove a bookmark, simply go to your dashboard, locate the item under your saved bookmarks list, and click **Remove**.

---

## 3. Capturing Personal Notes
Active note-taking is crucial to absorbing deep wisdom.
*   **Create a Note**: Click **New Note** on the dashboard. Add a title and summarize your thoughts.
*   **Automatic Search Discovery**: Once saved, your notes are automatically indexed. You can search for keywords within your notes using the global search bar on the portal.

---

## 4. Staying Consistent with Goals and Reminders
*   **Create Study Goals**: Go to the Goals section, click **Set Study Goal**, define your target hours, and update your progress as you study. Reach 100% to earn a celebratory milestone badge!
*   **Enable Learning Reminders**: Schedule study reminders on your dashboard to receive automated notifications and keep your daily learning streak alive.
