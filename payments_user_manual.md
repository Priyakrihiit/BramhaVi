# User Manual — Payments, Wallet & Subscription Platform
**Sprint 23 — BrahmaVidya Galaxy**

Welcome to the Payments and Wallet portal! This guide shows how to interact with your balances and billing services.

## 1. Accessing your Wallet
*   Navigate to the `/payments` path in your browser dashboard.
*   The **Wallet Tab** shows your virtual point balance (VIDYA coins).
*   Use the **Deposit Form** to add funds.
*   Use the **Transfer Form** to send credits to another student by specifying their email address or Wallet ID.

## 2. Purchasing Subscriptions & Memberships
*   Open the **Subscriptions Tab** to view plan tiers:
    *   **Student Premium** (₹99/month): Standard certificates access.
    *   **Teacher Premium** (₹299/month): Live class tools.
    *   **Institute Bundle** (₹999/month): Multi-seat licenses.
*   Click **Choose Tier Plan** to enroll in a subscription membership.

## 3. Order Checkout and Promo Vouchers
*   During cart checkout, enter active codes (e.g. `DISCOUNT20`) in the promo code block.
*   Click **Apply** to recalculate totals and verify discount rates.
*   Review the GST 18% splits before confirming the checkout.

## 4. Invoices & Receipts download
*   Open the **Invoices Tab** to list all past checkout sheets.
*   Click **Download TXT** to save a local print of your tax statement.
*   Click **Quick View** to inspect details directly on screen.
