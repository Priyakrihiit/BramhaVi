# BrahmaVidya Galaxy — Teacher Portal User Manual

Welcome to the BrahmaVidya Galaxy Teacher Portal! This manual guides you through using the Teacher Workspace.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1. Login and Access
1.  Navigate to the login portal.
2.  Enter your verified teacher credentials (email and password).
3.  Once authenticated, your role is verified, and you are redirected to the **Teacher Workspace**.

---

## 2. Managing Your Courses

### 2.1. Curriculum Curation (Course Builder)
1.  From the sidebar, click on **Course Builder**.
2.  Select the Course you are instructing.
3.  You can view the nested structure (Chapters, Topics, Lessons).
4.  To add a lesson: Click **Add Node**, select **LESSON**, upload your markdown materials or video links, and click **Save**.

### 2.2. Scheduling Live Stream Classes
1.  Navigate to **Live Classes**.
2.  Click **Schedule Class**, enter the start time and duration, and save.
3.  The system automatically sends notification alerts to all enrolled students.

---

## 3. Student Evaluation (Grading Submissions)

1.  Go to **Grading Hub**.
2.  Here you will find a list of student submissions categorized as "Awaiting Evaluation".
3.  Click on a submission to view the student's work side-by-side.
4.  Enter the numerical grade score and type text comments in the feedback section.
5.  Click **Submit Grade**. The student is immediately alerted.

---

## 4. Configuring Payments & Wallets

1.  Navigate to **My Wallet**.
2.  Click **Settings** to set up your payout preferences.
3.  Choose Stripe or PayPal and enter your details (e.g. PayPal email or Stripe account ID `acct_`).
4.  Your dashboard updates with your Month-to-Date Net Revenue.
