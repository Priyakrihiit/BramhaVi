# Business Requirement Document (BRD) - BrahmaVidya Galaxy

## 1. Executive Summary
**BrahmaVidya Galaxy** is an enterprise-grade Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) Educational Platform. It operates as a unified hybrid Content Management System (CMS) and Learning Management System (LMS) designed for global operations. The core business value lies in its **Content-Agnostic Engine Architecture** which eliminates reliance on development teams for everyday educational operations, structural site changes, or course catalog adjustments.

---

## 2. Business Objectives & Goals
1. **Reduce Time-to-Market**: Enable administrative personnel to configure landing pages, structural courses, and localized pricing tiers within minutes without writing code.
2. **Minimize Operational Overhead**: Deliver a complete administrative dashboard ("Control Center") that allows visual control over the site menu, roles, layout blocks, and certification templates.
3. **Enable Strategic Monetization**: Support automated billing splits, custom coupons, platform fee routing, and student wallets to support a vibrant creator economy for educators.
4. **AI-Enabled Scalability**: Automate curriculum generation and quiz creation utilizing localized AI models to reduce course-building friction for instructors by over 80%.

---

## 3. Product Vision & Value Proposition
Standard LMS tools enforce a rigid schema and static design, forcing institutions to spend thousands on custom development when they need custom pages or multi-tiered curricula.

BrahmaVidya Galaxy offers a unique value proposition:
- **Absolute Sovereign Control**: The system layout is entirely dictated by the database. If an administrator wants to change the landing page, add an interactive banner, or create a brand new custom page structure, they compose it visually in the Control Center.
- **Hierarchical Path Versatility**: Support for deep hierarchies (e.g., Degree -> Specialization -> Course -> Module -> Lesson -> Task) rather than standard flat course designs.
- **Sovereign Cryptographic Verification**: Native certificate issuing with digital signatures verifiable by anyone globally via QR code, removing dependencies on third-party verification companies.

---

## 4. Key Business Stakeholders
- **Platform Owners (SaaS Admins)**: Manage global subscription tiers, platform commissions, globally approved templates, and service-level configurations.
- **Partner Institutions & School Operators**: Host white-labeled school portals, configure unique navigation menus, set up custom roles, and track global revenue shares.
- **Instructors / Creators**: Authors of educational blocks who upload modular material, construct paths, publish quizzes, and receive automatic wallet deposits.
- **Learners / End-Users**: Direct consumers purchasing degrees, learning through modular interfaces, interacting with AI coaches, and earning verifiable achievements.

---

## 5. Monetization & Financial Engine
The platform includes an enterprise financial ledger supporting:
- **Direct Purchases**: One-time course or degree payments.
- **Platform Fee Splits**: Automatic percentage-based commissions routed to the platform treasury, with remaining funds deposited directly into the Instructor's wallet.
- **Institutional Wallets**: Dynamic bookkeeping system with ledger logs detailing credit, debit, and transfer transactions.
- **Promo Codes & Discount Engines**: Admin-defined campaign constraints mapped to courses.

---

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **CMS/LMS Setup Speed**: Measuring the duration of creating and launching a custom page and a multi-modular course (Target: < 10 minutes).
- **Core Page Load Latency**: Page render under 1.5s globally to maximize student retention and acquisition rates.
- **AI Utility Score**: Percentage of course structures generated by Vidya AI that are successfully accepted/modified by teachers (Target: > 75%).
- **Certification Authenticity Integrity**: Zero fraudulent certificates bypassed by the public QR cryptographic validation endpoint.
