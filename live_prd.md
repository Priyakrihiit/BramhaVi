# Live Classes Platform — Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Sprint 22 — Phase 9 Documentation**

## 1. Product Vision & Goals
Provide a highly engaging, stable, and low-latency virtual learning workspace within the BrahmaVidya Galaxy platform. Instructors must be able to schedule broadcasts, launch instant classes, share interactive canvas boards, deploy instant multiple-choice polls, and archive lectures automatically.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Instructors/Teachers**: Create, manage, and deliver live lectures.
*   **Scholars/Students**: Attend classes, participate in chats/polls, and replay archives.

## 3. Core Functional Requirements
1.  **Scheduling & Instancy**: Integrated forms to schedule future live events linked to course nodes or launch instant classrooms.
2.  **WebRTC Streaming Room**: High-fidelity video streaming canvas with presenter/attendee controls.
3.  **Active Attendance Logging**: Automated duration-tracking check-ins and check-outs synced to backend database tables.
4.  **Collaboration Tools**: Interactive whiteboard canvas and live text messaging boards.
5.  **Interactive Polls**: Real-time multi-choice vote casting with aggregated responses.
6.  **Recordings & Summaries**: Automatic MP4 archives compilation and AI-generated lecture summaries.
