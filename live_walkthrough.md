# Live Classes Platform — Step-by-Step Walkthrough
**Sprint 22 — Phase 9 Documentation**

## 1. Class Scheduling & Calendar Sync
1.  Navigate to teacher workspace dashboard ➔ **Live Classrooms**.
2.  Press **Schedule Broadcast**, enter details, and submit.
3.  Tap the **Academic Profile** or calendar timeline to verify that the schedule has been synced to calendar schedules.

## 2. In-Classroom Collaboration
1.  Click **Launch Broadcast Room** on the scheduled lecture.
2.  Click **Activate Live Stream**.
3.  Draw canvas items on the Whiteboard.
4.  Launch an interactive multi-choice Poll. Students submit choice votes, and response totals display dynamically.
5.  Type messages inside the **Live Chat** console.

## 3. Session End & Archives Replay
1.  Instructor clicks **End Stream** inside room.
2.  Exit room. The class displays as `COMPLETED`.
3.  Click **Play Archive** to retrieve the MP4 replay and AI summary.
