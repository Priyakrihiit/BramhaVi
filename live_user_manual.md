# Live Classes Platform — User Manual
**Sprint 22 — Phase 9 Documentation**

## 1. For Instructors
*   **Schedule a Class**: Tap **Schedule Broadcast** on the top right of the dashboard. Pick the subject, scheduled date/time, and name the meeting.
*   **Launch Broadcast**: Select **Launch Broadcast Room** next to the scheduled item. Click **Activate Live Stream** to go live.
*   **Create Polls**: Fill in the **Create Poll** form on the right sidebar and click **Publish Poll** to gather responses.

## 2. For Students
*   **Join Room**: Tap **Join Classroom** next to any active class listed on the dashboard.
*   **Collaborate**: Draw on the whiteboard or type questions inside the chat panel.
*   **Vote**: Submit choice selections inside the interactive polls panel.
